
# Supported Conversions

<br>

## Output

*The HSL(A) output is within the following ranges.*

<br>

| Hue | Saturation | Lightness | Alpha |
|:---:|:----------:|:---------:|:-----:|
| `0 - 360` | `0 - 100` | `0 - 100` | `0 - 255`

<br>
<br>

## RGB ( A )

All channels are numbers in the range of  `0 - 255`

<br>

|   | RGB | RGBA |
|:-:|:---:|:----:|
| **Array**     | ( [ R , G , B ] ) | ( [ R , G , B , A ] )
| **Parameter** | ( R , G , B ) | ( R , G , B , A )

<br>
